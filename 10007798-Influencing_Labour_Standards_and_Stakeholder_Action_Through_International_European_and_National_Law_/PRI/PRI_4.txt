"Social dialogue is linked to higher productivity, cost-efficiency, and overall business stability." (p. 40)

Business Growth: By demonstrating the link between social dialogue and productivity, the study can influence businesses to adopt or improve their labor relations practices, resulting in enhanced economic performance.
